How to Play CHALOO

Objective

Be the first player to collect 3 complete property sets of different colors.

On Your Turn

  1. Draw 2 cards (5 if you start with an empty hand).
  2. Play up to 3 cards from your hand:
    • Put money/action cards in your Bank.
    • Put property cards in your Property collection.
    • Play action cards into the center.
  3. At the end of your turn, discard if you have more than 7 cards.
